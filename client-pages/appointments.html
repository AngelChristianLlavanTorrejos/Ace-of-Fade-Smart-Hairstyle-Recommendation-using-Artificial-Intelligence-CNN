Appointment Policy
Please read our policies carefully before booking an appointment.
  1. All appointments must be booked at least 3 days in advance.
  2. You may book an appointment up to 1 month ahead of the current date.
  3. Clients with an existing pending appointment must complete or cancel it before booking another.
  4. Appointments are available only between 08:00 A.M and 05:00 P.M.
  5. Each appointment is exactly 1 hour long.
